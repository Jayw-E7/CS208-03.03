Meridian, Idaho

Location

Meridian is a city located in southwest Idaho in the United States.

About

History

The town of Meridian was established in 1891. In 1903, it was incorporated as a village, and then became a city in 1909.

Geography

Meridian has a land area of 27 square miles, with the region being a mostly flat plain.

Population

As of 2020, Meridian's population was around 118,000 people, and now it is projected to be around 140,000.

Weather

Expect cold winters and hot, dry summers with little rainfall all year around.

Things to do

  • Visit the Village at Meridian
  • Walk along the Boise River Greenbelt
  • Go to Roaring Springs or Wahooz
  • Visit downtown
